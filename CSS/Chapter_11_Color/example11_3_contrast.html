Best Practices

  • Text is more difficult to read with low contrast, especially for those with visual impairments or color blindness
  • High contrast is easier to read
  • A light background with dark text is easiest to read
  • For long spans of text it can help to reduce the contrast a little
  • For dark backgrounds and light text it can help to increase spacing between lines and font weight
